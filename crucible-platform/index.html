The Investigation Pipeline

From signal to resolved incident — humans where it matters.

Six deterministic stages, every time. Each stage is observable, attributable, and auditable.

1
Ingest
Signals stream in from the security stack and are captured and de-duplicated.
2
Playbook match
The right investigation playbook is auto-selected for the signal type.
3
Triage
AI classifies severity, category and false-positive likelihood, then routes.
4
Investigate
Specialist agents query historical data, build timelines and gather evidence.
5
Coagulate
Related signals are clustered into a single unified incident.
6
Respond
A concrete response is proposed, scaled to confidence and risk; production-impacting actions wait for human approval.
